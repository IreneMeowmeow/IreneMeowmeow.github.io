About

This is Pirate Cat, a comic I have been working on for quite a while now! It's about Alwyn over here, and his crew. But no one really calls him that, he'd much prefer you'd just call him Cat.

He'll gladly take you along on his adventures, though probably with a bit of pissing and moaning. I can't promise they'll always be dramatic or interesting. But I can certainly guarantee that they'll find a way to fuck it up, no matter how easy their tasks may be.

But then again. So far he's managed to crawl out of his little messes. So I wouldn't be too worried about him.

and Thank you for lending your eyeballs to my little project!

- Irene, author and artist
